Loading Practice Clinics

Our Loading Practice Clinics are designed to help horses build confidence, reduce anxiety, and develop safe, reliable loading behaviour. Whether your horse has had a setback, refuses to load, or simply needs confidence-building, these sessions provide calm, structured support tailored to your horse’s needs.

Each clinic takes place at your location using your own trailer, ensuring your horse learns in the exact environment they will be expected to load in. Sessions include groundwork, confidence-building exercises, and progressive loading practice.
