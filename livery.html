Livery Services

At Luomara, our livery services are built around natural horse care, ethical management, and a calm, supportive environment. Both our grass and track systems encourage movement, social interaction, and natural equine behaviour, giving horses the freedom to roam, explore, and choose how they spend their day. The fields include a wooded area for natural shelter and multiple water points for fresh, clean drinking water. We’re always looking for ways to improve, and we’re planning to add hay feeding stations, a sand pit, a mineral‑selection area, logs for enrichment, and a couple of additional shelter options to make the environment even more enriching and horse‑friendly.
