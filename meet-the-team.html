Meet the Team

At Luomara, our team is united by a shared passion for ethical horse care, natural management, and creating a calm, supportive environment for both horses and owners.

Suvi

Founder of Luomara and dedicated equine professional. Suvi brings compassion, experience, and a deep commitment to natural horse care. Passionate carriage driver and endurance rider who breeds friesian and shire horses at small scale. Currently training to become a qualified equine podiatrist and riding instructor.

Steve

Experienced team member helping maintain the track ang grazing systems, supporting daily care, and ensuring every horse receives consistent attention. Looking into offering equine therapy options with intense background in mental health and residential child care.

Visiting Professionals

Including farriers, podiatrists, bodyworkers, and veterinary partners who support the wellbeing of every horse at Luomara.
