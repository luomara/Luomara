Equine Podiatry

Suvi is currently undergoing training to become a fully qualified equine podiatrist and will be offering consultations and trimming within her local area. Our approach to hoof care is grounded in evidence‑based practice, natural biomechanics, and a deep understanding of the horse as a whole. We believe that healthy movement, appropriate surfaces, and correct trimming form the foundation of long‑term soundness.

Natural Hoof Care

Focused on balance, comfort, and long‑term function. We work with the horse’s natural movement patterns to support healthy hoof development.

Track System Benefits

Our track environment encourages movement and supports natural wear patterns.

Collaborative Care

We work alongside vets, bodyworkers, and owners to ensure each horse receives holistic, consistent support.
